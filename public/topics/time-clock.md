# Time Reading Quiz Game for 6-Year-Olds

A fun, step-by-step game to learn telling time!  
**Target Age**: 6 years old  
**Structure**: Lesson (explain + examples) → Quiz (5 questions, easy to hard) → Progress to next lesson.  
**Tips**: Use colorful clock images, animations, or toy clocks in your app. Reward correct answers with stars or sounds.

## Lesson 1: Seconds, Minutes, and Hours

**Explanation**:  
Time has small units like seconds (fast tick-tock, like a heartbeat), minutes (60 seconds = 1 minute, like waiting for a song), and hours (60 minutes = 1 hour, like from breakfast to lunch). A day has 24 hours!

**Examples**:

- Brushing teeth: 2 minutes.
- School day: 6 hours.
- Running: 10 seconds.

### Quiz 1

1. How many seconds in 1 minute?  
   a) 10 b) **60** c) 100

2. How many minutes in 1 hour?  
   a) **60** b) 24 c) 10

3. What takes about 1 hour?  
   a) **Watching a cartoon** b) Blinking c) Eating one candy

4. How many seconds to count "1-Mississippi"?  
   a) **1** b) 60 c) 24

5. True or False: A day has 24 hours.

## Lesson 2: Days and Basic Time Words

**Explanation**:  
7 days = 1 week. Time words: o'clock (full hour), half (halfway), quarter (1/4 of hour). Morning (AM), afternoon, evening, night.

**Examples**:

- Monday to Sunday = week.
- 3 o'clock = full hour.

### Quiz 2

1. How many days in a week?  
   a) 5 b) **7** c) 24

2. Koľko je "half past 2"?  
   a) 2:00 b) **2:30** c) 3:00

3. School is usually in the \_\_\_\_.  
   a) **Morning** b) Night c) Midnight

4. 15 minutes is a \_\_\_\_ of an hour.  
   a) Half b) **Quarter** c) Full

5. Days in March? (Basic fact)  
   **31** [web:15 from prior search]

## Lesson 3: Digital Clock Basics

**Explanation**:  
Digital clocks show numbers like a calculator: HH:MM (hours:minutes). No hands! 12-hour (AM/PM) or 24-hour format.

**Examples**:

- 09:00 = 9 AM.
- 14:30 = 2:30 PM.

### Quiz 3

1. What time is 3:00 on digital?  
   **03:00** or **15:00** (24-hr)

2. 12:00 is \_\_\_\_.  
   **Noon** (midday)

3. PM means \_\_\_\_.  
   **After noon**

4. Koľko je 7:45 PM?  
   **19:45** (24-hr)

5. Bedtime example: 8:00 PM = ?  
   **20:00** [web:8 from prior search]

## Lesson 4: Analog Clock - Hours and Half Hours

**Explanation**:  
Analog clocks have hands: short hour hand, long minute hand. Circle = 12 hours. Full hour: hour hand on number. Half past: minute hand on 6.

**Examples**:

- 2:00: Hour on 2, minute at 12.
- 4:30: Minute on 6.

### Quiz 4

1. Where is minute hand at 3:00?  
   **12**

2. 6:30 on analog: Minute hand on?  
   **6**

3. Hour hand at 10 o'clock?  
   **10**

4. What time: Hour on 5, minute on 12?  
   **5:00**

## Lesson 5: Analog Clock - Quarter Hours

**Explanation**:  
Quarter past (:15, minute on 3), quarter to (:45, minute on 9). Practice moving hands.

**Examples**:

- 2:15 = quarter past 2.
- 5:45 = quarter to 6.

### Quiz 5

1. Quarter past 4?  
   **4:15**

2. Minute hand on 3 = ? minutes past.  
   **15**

3. Quarter to 7?  
   **6:45**

4. What time: Minute on 9?  
   **Quarter to** the hour

## Lesson 6: Mixed Clocks and Word Problems

**Explanation**:  
Combine digital/analog. Read schedules: "What time is snack? 10:30."

**Examples**:

- Digital 11:45 = Analog quarter to 12.

### Quiz 6 (Hardest)

1. Digital 09:30 = Analog?  
   **Half past 9**

2. Recess at 2:45 PM = ?  
   **Quarter to 3**

3. How many hours from 8 AM to 12 PM?  
   **4**

4. Analog: Hour between 3-4, minute on 6?  
   **3:30**

5. Bedtime 60 minutes after 7:30 PM?  
   **8:30 PM** [web:14 from prior search]
